Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about purchases and ranks

How long do ranks last? +
All ranks are valid for 30 days from the purchase date. You'll receive a notification in-game when your rank is about to expire, giving you the option to renew.
How do I activate my rank after purchase? +
Your rank will be automatically activated within 5 minutes of a successful purchase. If your rank isn't activated after 10 minutes, please contact our support team via Discord.
Can I upgrade my rank? +
Yes! You can upgrade your rank at any time. When upgrading, you'll only pay the difference between your current rank and the new rank, and your remaining time will transfer to the new rank.
Are refunds available? +
We do not offer refunds for rank purchases. Please make sure you read all the rank features carefully before making a purchase.
Do ranks work on all servers? +
Yes, your rank and its benefits apply across all Ivonia Network servers and game modes, though some specific perks may vary depending on the game mode.
