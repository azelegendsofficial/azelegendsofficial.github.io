About IvoniaSmp

Our server's story and mission

IvoniaSmp started in 2020 as a small server for friends, but quickly grew into a thriving community of Minecraft enthusiasts from around the world.

Our mission is to provide a fun, safe, and creative environment where players can build, explore, and connect with others who share their passion for Minecraft.

We pride ourselves on our active moderation team, regular updates, and player-driven development. Many of our features and improvements come directly from player suggestions!

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about IvoniaSmp

How do I join the server?
To join IvoniaSmp, simply add our server address (play.ivoniasmp.com) to your Minecraft server list. We support Minecraft Java Edition version 1.19 and above.
Is the server free to play?
Yes, IvoniaSmp is completely free to play! We do offer optional donation ranks that provide cosmetic benefits, but all core gameplay features are available to everyone.
How can I protect my builds?
We use a claim system that allows you to protect your builds. Simply use a golden shovel to select the corners of your land, and follow the instructions in chat to complete your claim.
Are there any rules I should know about?
Yes, we have a few basic rules to ensure everyone has a good time: no griefing, no hacking/cheating, be respectful to other players, and no excessive profanity. You can view our complete rules on our Discord server.
How can I report a player or issue?
If you encounter any issues or need to report a player, you can use the /report command in-game, or contact a staff member on our Discord server. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.
